# 隱私權政策

At Breadth Software, Inc. (referred to as "BreadthApp" or "we"), we value your privacy and are committed to protecting
your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data when you
use any of the services provided by Breadth. By using our services, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

## Information We Collect and the Lawful Basis for Processing

We collect the minimum necessary personal information to provide our services effectively. The types of personal data we may collect include your name, email address, and other necessary information required for authentication and authorization.

Lawful Basis for Processing
We process your personal information based on the necessity for the performance of our contract with you to provide our services. If you do not provide the required information, we may not be able to deliver our services effectively.

## Data Processing Details
### Purpose of Processing

We process your data primarily to provide and improve our services. The specific purposes include:

- Authentication and Authorization: We use your personal information to authenticate and authorize access to our services.
- Communication: We may use your contact details to communicate with you about updates, security alerts, and support and administrative messages.
- Service Improvement: We use data from cookies and third-party analytics services to understand user behavior and enhance our services.
- Processing Activities: The processing activities include the collection, storage, organization, structuring, adaptation, retrieval, consultation, use, disclosure by transmission, dissemination, alignment or combination, restriction, erasure, and destruction of your personal data.

## Recipients of Personal Data
In providing our services, we may share your personal data with certain third parties, including our service providers who assist in delivering our services. We may also disclose your personal information to law enforcement agencies if required by law or to comply with a legal process. We will not sell or rent your personal data to any third parties.

## Automated Decision Making and Profiling
We do not use your personal data for automated decision-making or profiling purposes.

## Data Subject Rights
As a data subject, you have certain rights under applicable data protection laws, including the right to access, correct, update, or delete your personal data. You also have the right to object to the processing of your personal information, request the restriction of processing, or request the portability of your personal data. You can opt-out of receiving marketing communications from us at any time.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights or have any privacy-related concerns, please contact us at support@breadth.app. We will respond to your requests within a reasonable timeframe and in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

## Data Retention Period

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the services you have requested and for legitimate
legal or business purposes. We may retain your data for compliance with legal, contractual, or similar obligations
applicable to our business operations, resolving disputes, enforcing our rights, or maintaining accurate business and
financial records.

## Cross-Border Data Transfers

Your personal data may be transferred and stored on servers located outside your country of residence, including in the
United States. When we transfer your data to such jurisdictions, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to
protect your personal information.

## Use of Cookies

We use strictly necessary cookies to ensure secure authentication and authorization for our services. These cookies do
not contain any personal information and are deleted once you sign out of our service.

## se of Analytics Services

We use analytics services, such as Plausible and Application Insights, to collect non-personally identifiable data on
user behavior, page views, and usage patterns. These services help us improve our services, but they do not collect
personal information.

## About Google User Data

Access to Google User Data
BreadthApp only accesses certain Google account information with explicit user consent. When a user signs up BreadthApp,
they grant us access to certain account information, such as name, email address, and profile picture.

## Use of Google User Data

BreadthApp uses Google user data solely for the purpose of providing our services to the user. This includes enabling
the user to sign in to our app, and providing access to necessary features within BreadthApp.

## Storage of Google User Data

BreadthApp stores Google user data on secure servers located in data centers within the Private Network.

## Sharing of Google User Data

BreadthApp does not share Google user data with any third parties, except as required by law or to comply with legal
process

## Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required by law, to comply with
legal processes, or as necessary to provide our services.

## Data Security

We have implemented reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or
disclosure. These measures include secure communication protocols, private database networks, data isolation, password
encryption, and database encryption. We regularly review and update our security practices to ensure the protection of
your personal data.

## Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make any material changes, we will notify you by email or
by posting a notice on our website.
